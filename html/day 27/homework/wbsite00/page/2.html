Top 10 Famous Persons

  1. Jesus Christ: Central figure of Christianity, whose teachings and life have had an immense impact on Western civilization.

  2. Muhammad: Founder of Islam, whose teachings and leadership shaped the religion and influenced millions worldwide.

  3. Albert Einstein: Revolutionary physicist known for his theory of relativity and one of the most recognizable figures in science.

  4. William Shakespeare: Regarded as one of the greatest writers in the English language, his works continue to be studied and performed globally.

  5. Leonardo da Vinci: Renowned polymath of the Renaissance, known for his art (like the Mona Lisa) and contributions to science and engineering.

  6. Nelson Mandela: Anti-apartheid revolutionary and former President of South Africa, celebrated for his role in ending apartheid and promoting reconciliation.

  7. Mother Teresa: Catholic nun and missionary known for her humanitarian work, particularly in Kolkata (Calcutta), India.

  8. Mahatma Gandhi: Leader of the Indian independence movement against British rule, known for his philosophy of nonviolent resistance

  9. Marilyn Monroe: Iconic actress and symbol of beauty and glamour in the 1950s and 1960s.

  10. Michael Jackson: Pop music icon known as the "King of Pop," whose music and influence transcended generations.
